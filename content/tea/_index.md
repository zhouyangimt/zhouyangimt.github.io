If you like coffee, I'd suggest you try Chinese tea. If you don't like coffee, I'd still suggest you try Chinese tea. In southern China, tea and rice are often spoken of together as *chá mǐ* (茶米) — tea-rice, two staples so fundamental they share a breath.
